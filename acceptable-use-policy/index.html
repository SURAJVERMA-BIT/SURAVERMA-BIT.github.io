Acceptable Use Policy

In Effect as of May 29, 2026

Welcome to the Acceptable Use Policy (AUP) for XiteAI Technologies Pvt. Ltd. This framework dictates the permitted and restricted uses of our educational AI platform, ensuring a secure, equitable, and constructive ecosystem for all users.

By accessing the XiteAI platform (chat.xiteai.com), APIs, or related services (collectively the "Services"), you agree to adhere to this AUP. Violations may result in platform suspension or permanent account termination.

A. Core Usage Principles

XiteAI is architected to facilitate academic rigor, advanced research, and professional productivity. You must use our Services in a manner consistent with civil discourse and international law.

1. Academic Integrity

While XiteAI provides robust tools like the PYQ Analyzer and MCQ Studio, it must not be deployed to undermine institutional honor codes. You agree not to use the platform for direct contract cheating, submitting AI-generated material as original academic work where prohibited, or attempting to compromise proctored assessments.

2. Lawful Execution

The Services must not be utilized to propagate, execute, or plan illegal acts. This includes, but is not limited to, generating instructions for the creation of illicit substances, facilitating financial fraud, phishing, or breaching cryptographic security systems.

B. Prohibited Content and Behaviors

To ensure a safe ecosystem, XiteAI enforces absolute restrictions against specific query categories. You shall not submit prompts intended to:

  • Generate Hate Speech or Harassment: Content that denigrates, incites violence against, or threatens individuals or groups based on race, religion, sexual orientation, or disability.
  • Produce Non-Consensual Explicit Material: Any attempt to solicit explicit imagery, CSAM, or non-consensual deepfakes.
  • Engage in Malicious Social Engineering: Using our models to craft convincing phishing payloads, spam campaigns, or automated social media disinformation bots.
  • Circumvent Safety Protocols: Actively attempting to "jailbreak," prompt-inject, or otherwise bypass the multi-layered safety guardrails instituted by XiteAI or its underlying foundry providers (e.g., Anthropic, OpenAI).

C. Intellectual Property Respect

You may not leverage the XiteAI platform to systematically scrape or ingest copyrighted data without proper authorization. Do not request the verbatim reproduction of paywalled articles, proprietary codebase snippets belonging to protected third parties, or commercially licensed datasets.

D. Operational Integrity

Protecting the uptime and stability of our API gateways and compute queues is critical.

  • No Automated Abuse: You are prohibited from deploying unauthorized scrapers, headless browsers, or brute-force scripts against our interface to extract generated content or bypass rate limits.
  • Context Limit Compliance: Repeatedly flooding the context window with malicious or recursive tokens (token stuffing/bloating) designed to degrade server performance will trigger automatic defensive bans.

E. Enforcement and Reporting

XiteAI relies on both automated moderation heuristic cascades and manual human reviews (when a severe flag is triggered). We reserve the right to temporarily quarantine accounts under investigation. If you encounter outputs or suspect usage that violates this policy, you are encouraged to immediately escalate the issue via our reporting pathways.

For urgent abuse reports or policy inquiries:

  • Trust & Safety Team: abuse@xiteai.com
  • Corporate Entity: XiteAI Technologies Pvt. Ltd.
