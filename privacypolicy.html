Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 27, 2025

1. Introduction

At XiteAI Technologies (“XiteAI,” “we,” “us,” or “our”), your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services, including our website, mobile applications (iOS and Android), and AI-powered features driven by our intelligent query-routing system, Maestro, and our high-performance AI architecture, SuperXite. We collect minimal data, processed through trusted third-party providers or stored locally at your discretion, ensuring transparency and control. By using XiteAI, you agree to this policy. Contact us with questions using the details below.

2. Information We Collect

XiteAI collects only essential data to deliver our services, prioritizing minimal exposure and user control. Data is processed via trusted partners or stored locally/cloud-based as per your preferences.

a. Account Information

We collect limited personal information when you voluntarily create an account, such as:

  • Name and email address
  • Account credentials
  • Billing details (processed securely via third-party providers)
  • Optional chat preferences (stored locally or in secure cloud storage)

If you log in via third-party services (e.g., Google, Apple), we may receive basic account details like your username or email, as directed by you.

b. Payment Information

For paid services, we use third-party processors (e.g., Stripe, Apple App Store) to handle payment details securely. XiteAI does not store payment information.

c. Communication Data

When you contact us via email, support forms, or social media, we collect information you provide, such as your name, email, and message content, to respond and improve our services.

d. User Content

Inputs like text prompts or chat settings, and outputs generated by our AI, are processed minimally. You control whether this data is stored locally or in secure cloud storage. Avoid including sensitive personal information in prompts.

e. Feedback Data

Feedback, such as ratings on AI responses, is collected to enhance our services. This data is anonymized and used solely for improving XiteAI’s performance.

f. Social Media and Public Data

Interactions on our social media pages (e.g., X, LinkedIn) or public features like forums may be collected as provided by you or platform hosts. We use anonymized analytics to understand engagement.

g. Technical Data

We collect minimal technical data for functionality, including:

  • IP address (used temporarily, not stored long-term)
  • Device and browser details
  • Usage data (e.g., pages visited, session duration)

This data is processed via trusted providers and not retained beyond operational needs.

h. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to enable seamless login, chat functionality, and anonymized analytics. You can manage cookies via browser settings, but disabling them may limit features.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to deliver a secure, efficient, and personalized experience, including:

  • Routing queries via Maestro to specialized AI providers for accurate responses.
  • Powering SuperXite’s high-performance AI outputs.
  • Processing payments securely for subscriptions or usage-based plans.
  • Enabling customizable chat experiences with local or secure cloud storage.
  • Sending essential account or support communications.
  • Analyzing anonymized data to refine our platform.
  • Ensuring security and compliance with legal obligations.

We do not use your data for marketing or sell it to third parties.

4. Sharing Information

XiteAI shares minimal data, only when necessary, under strict privacy protections:

  • Trusted Providers: Maestro routes queries to AI providers (e.g., OpenRouter) for processing. Review their privacy policies for details.
  • Secure Vendors: Payment processors, cloud storage providers, or support services receive limited data under confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: We may share data to comply with laws or protect XiteAI and its users.
  • No Sales: We never sell or share your data for marketing purposes.

5. AI Integration and Privacy

Our AI features, powered by Maestro and SuperXite, prioritize privacy:

  • Query Routing: Maestro sends queries to trusted AI providers without XiteAI retaining inputs or outputs.
  • No Training: We request that providers do not use your data for model training, though you should verify their policies.
  • Private Chats: Custom chat modes are isolated, stored locally or in secure cloud storage for privacy.
  • Minimal Exposure: SuperXite’s architecture ensures efficient, privacy-focused interactions.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption (SSL/TLS) for data transmission.
  • Minimal data retention, with Maestro routing queries securely.
  • Local or encrypted cloud storage options for user control.
  • Regular security audits of third-party providers.

While we strive for maximum security, no internet transmission is fully secure. Users assume inherent risks.

7. Children’s Privacy

XiteAI is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect their data. Parents must consent for users aged 13–17, and we encourage monitoring their use due to potential mature content.

8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your data.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing.
  • Request data portability.
  • Appeal decisions regarding your data requests.

To exercise these rights, contact us at incxiteai@gmail.com. We’ll respond within legal timeframes, verifying your identity as needed. Correcting AI outputs may be limited by technical constraints.

9. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary for service delivery, legal compliance, or security. Chat data is stored locally or in secure cloud storage, deleted within 30 days of account closure unless legally required.

10. Third-Party Links

Our services may link to third-party sites (e.g., OpenRouter). We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Review their policies before sharing data.

11. Policy Updates

We may update this policy as XiteAI evolves, notifying you via email or app updates. Continued use after changes implies acceptance.
