# Responsible AI Policy

**Effective: May 29, 2026**

XiteAI is committed to the safe, ethical, and transparent deployment of intelligent systems. We have structured our multi-agent architecture (SuperXite Lite) to align with international safety standards.

## 1. Misuse Prevention
We employ robust safety filters to actively reject prompts designed to generate illegal, hateful, self-harming, or malicious content (such as phishing payloads or malware).

## 2. Mitigating Bias & Hallucination
We continuously calibrate our system prompts to neutralize harmful biases inherent to base models and to reduce hallucination rates. However, AI is probabilistic, and human oversight is mandatory for all critical outputs.

## 3. Human Oversight
We rely on aggregated anomaly detection and Human-In-The-Loop (HITL) auditing on strictly anonymized metadata to monitor output quality and platform safety, ensuring XiteAI remains a constructive educational environment.
