# Support & Digital Delivery Policy

**Effective: May 29, 2026**

## 1. Digital Delivery
XiteAI operates solely as a digital Software-as-a-Service (SaaS). Upon successful authorization of your payment token, your account tier is instantly provisioned. 
- You will gain immediate access to heightened context windows and premium routing features. 
- There are no physical goods to ship.

## 2. Requesting Support
Our support framework operates asynchronously via email.
- **Billing & Account Support:** Email `support@xiteai.com`. We target a 48-hour response window for foundational access issues.
- **Bug Reports & Feature Requests:** While deeply appreciated, bug submissions are triaged by our engineering team and do not guarantee personalized technical assistance or immediate patches.

## 3. Service Downtime
During periods of unscheduled maintenance, we provide operational updates on our Status page. Support response times may be temporarily delayed during platform-wide upgrades.
