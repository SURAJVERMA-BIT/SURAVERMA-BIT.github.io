# Status & Uptime Policy

**Effective: May 29, 2026**

## 1. MVP & Beta Contingencies
During this Minimum Viable Product (MVP) and Beta expansion phase, XiteAI operates on a "best-effort" availability standard. We are scaling rapidly and **do not** currently provide a financially backed Service Level Agreement (SLA) or a guaranteed 99.99% uptime.

## 2. Maintenance and Outages

- **No Liability:** You agree that XiteAI is not liable for data access delays, missed academic deadlines, or unsubmitted work resulting from unscheduled system downtime or API degraded performance.
