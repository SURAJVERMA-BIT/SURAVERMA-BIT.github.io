# File & Prompt Processing Policy

**Effective: May 29, 2026**

## 1. Prompt and Chat History
To enable you to seamlessly resume past studies and interact with persistent personas, XiteAI stores your chat transcripts in securely encrypted databases.

## 2. Uploaded Files
Files uploaded to the workspace (e.g., PDFs, DOCXs for PYQ Analyzer) are stored securely to enable contextual grounding for the AI.
- **User Control:** You can manually delete any uploaded file directly from your workspace.
- **Destruction:** Files deleted by you are permanently purged from our cloud storage.

## 3. No Model Training
XiteAI **does not** train its own foundation models using your personal prompts, uploaded assignments, research papers, or generated Cheat Sheets. Your intellectual property remains securely yours. Data is only processed to provide you with the immediate output you requested.
