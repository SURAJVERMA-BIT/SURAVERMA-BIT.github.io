# Children & Minors Policy

**Effective: May 29, 2026**

XiteAI provides advanced educational toolkits tailored for academic growth, but it is strictly regulated regarding minor access.

## 1. Age Thresholds
You must be at least **13 years old** (in the US) or the mandated age of digital consent in your local jurisdiction (e.g., up to 16 in certain EU states) to hold an independent XiteAI account.

## 2. Parental Guidance & Consent
Users operating below these legal thresholds may only interface with the platform under the active supervision and explicit consent of a legal parent or guardian. 

## 3. Enforcement
XiteAI does not knowingly collect personal data from unsupervised children. Unverified accounts established by under-aged users attempting to bypass these safety restrictions will be terminated immediately and their data purged upon discovery.
