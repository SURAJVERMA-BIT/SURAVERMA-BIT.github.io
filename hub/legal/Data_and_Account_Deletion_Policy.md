# Data & Account Deletion Policy

**Effective: May 29, 2026**

## 1. Complete Account Deletion
You maintain total sovereignty over your digital footprint. By navigating to **Account Settings > Danger Zone > Delete Account**, you can permanently erase your account.
- **Immediate Action:** Your login identity, active sessions, and personal identifiers are wiped immediately from our active databases.
- **Support & Billing Data:** Support tickets and billing histories are retained in siloed databases for up to 7 years exclusively to comply with international tax and legal obligations.

## 2. Managing Chats and Files
- **Chats:** You can delete individual chats at any time. Once deleted, they are immediately stripped from your context window and queued for permanent purging.
- **Files:** Any documents uploaded for analysis can be deleted from your file interface. Upon deletion, they are destroyed from our temporary cloud storage.

## 3. Third-Party Propagation
Aggregated models processed securely by our zero-retention API partners (like Anthropic or OpenAI) do not retain your data downstream. Erasing your local XiteAI record ensures a complete digital wipe.
