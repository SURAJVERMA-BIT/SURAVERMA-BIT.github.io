# Fair Usage & Rate Limit Policy

**Effective: May 29, 2026**

## 1. Ecosystem Protection
To prevent service degradation and ensure high availability for all users, XiteAI heavily enforces structural rate limits and contextual token ceilings. AI Software-as-a-Service platforms incur massive compute costs, and this policy protects our infrastructure from abuse.

## 2. Acceptable Rhythms
While we advertise generous contextual limits, anomalous behavior will trigger instant defensive rate-limits. Prohibited behaviors include:
- Deploying automated scraping scripts.
- Multi-tab recursive token flooding.
- API abuse designed to artificially drain compute resources.

## 3. Account Actions
Severe "token stuffing" or automation attacks will result in an absolute IP and account ban without a refund. XiteAI reserves the right to dynamically calibrate operational limits per user tier based on global cluster load to ensure equitable access for genuine users.
