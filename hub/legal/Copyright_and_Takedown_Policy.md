# Copyright & Takedown Policy (DMCA)

**Effective: May 29, 2026**

XiteAI honors global intellectual property frameworks, including the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA). Because users can upload files (PDFs, assignments, books) for analysis, we maintain a strict takedown mechanism to prevent rights infringement.

## 1. Submitting a Complaint
If you believe a user has uploaded and is actively distributing your copyrighted material through public sharing spaces on our platform, provide our Legal Desk (`legal@xiteai.com`) with:
1. Proof of intellectual ownership.
2. The specific URLs or identifiers of the infringing content on XiteAI.
3. A formal declaration under penalty of perjury that the use is unauthorized.

## 2. Enforcement
Upon receiving a valid DMCA request, XiteAI will promptly remove or disable access to the infringing material. We operate under a "three-strikes" recidivism policy for intellectual property abuse, which will result in permanent user bans.
