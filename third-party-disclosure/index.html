Third-Party AI Provider Disclosure

In Effect as of May 29, 2026

XiteAI operates as an advanced multi-modal routing ecosystem. To provide state-of-the-art responses, your prompts are mapped asynchronously to specialized third-party inference engines.

1. Active Foundation Models

Depending on your selected persona and subscription tier, your explicit prompts are securely transmitted to top-tier providers, including but not limited to:

  • OpenAI (e.g., GPT-4o, GPT-5 variants)
  • Anthropic (e.g., Claude 3.5/4 variants)
  • xAI (e.g., Grok ecosystem)

2. Zero-Retention Data Agreements

When your data leaves XiteAI servers and interfaces with third-party APIs, it is transmitted strictly under enterprise data agreements. Our providers are contractually bound to function as "zero-retention" conduits. This means they do not utilize your prompts or private files to train their base models.
