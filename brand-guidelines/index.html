XiteAI Brand Identity

In Effect as of May 29, 2026

Welcome to the official Brand Identity and Usage Guidelines for XiteAI Technologies Pvt. Ltd.

The XiteAI brand marks, typographic systems, and visual layouts are proprietary assets. This comprehensive framework dictates how external media, affiliates, educational partners, and public users may interact with and display our corporate IP. Strict adherence maintains the structural integrity and premium aesthetic of the XiteAI platform.

A. Trademarks and Corporate Marks

Our designated nomenclature, logos, and specific product modules (including, but not limited to, SuperXite Lite, PYQ Analyzer, and MCQ Studio) are legally recognized trademarks of XiteAI Technologies. You are granted a limited, revocable, and non-exclusive right to utilize our brand marks solely to reference our platform.

Usage Prohibitions

  • No Modification: You must not distort, skew, rotate, or alter the aspect ratio of the primary XiteAI logo under any circumstance.
  • No Color Alteration: Do not shift the hex-codes, apply gradients, or invert our core branding away from the approved high-contrast monochromatic schemes.
  • No Endorsement Spoofing: Incorporating our logo in a manner that falsely implies partnership, formal endorsement, or joint-venture sponsorship by XiteAI without an explicit, signed corporate agreement is strictly forbidden.

B. Clear Space and Structural Scaling

The XiteAI logo was engineered for high legibility across dynamic media. To preserve its impact:

  • Always maintain a dedicated Clear Space surrounding the primary logomark equal to 50% of the logo's height. Do not crowd the brand mark with external text or secondary graphics.
  • Do not scale the primary logo down to a resolution where the typography becomes illegible or pixelated. Use the designated XiteAI minimal icon ("favicon" format) for ultra-compact applications.

C. Representation of AI Provider Partnerships

XiteAI is a sophisticated routing platform that interfaces with underlying foundation models (e.g., OpenAI's GPT-5, Anthropic's Claude, xAI's Grok). When discussing our technical architecture publicly, it must be explicitly framed as an integration.

You may not misrepresent XiteAI as the proprietary author or owner of the external provider's LLM engine. Proper attribution syntax: "XiteAI harnesses the power of [Provider Model] within its educational ecosystem."

D. Typography and UI Aesthetic

Our platform relies heavily on a clean, professional, and accessible UX structure leveraging the Inter font stack. When attempting to emulate or visually align a partnered widget with XiteAI, we strongly encourage adopting a high-contrast, dark-mode-first aesthetic with minimalist UI dividers and clean typographic hierarchy.

Submitting Brand Inquiries

If you require high-resolution SVG vector assets or have questions regarding a potential cobranding integration, our Design and Strategy team must issue formal approval.

  • Brand Asset Requests: design@xiteai.com
  • Corporate Partner Desk: partners@xiteai.com
