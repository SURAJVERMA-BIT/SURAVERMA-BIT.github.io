Cookie Policy

Last Updated: January 2, 2026

1. About This Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how XiteAI Technologies (“XiteAI,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) uses cookies and similar technologies to operate our AI-driven platform, including our website XiteAI Home and mobile apps (iOS and Android), collectively referred to as the “Platform.” Currently in its waitlist phase, the Platform is set to launch in 2026. For information on how we handle personal data, please see our Privacy Policy.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files stored on your device when you interact with our Platform, such as visiting our website or using our apps. We also use local storage to save data directly on your device. These technologies support seamless functionality and a secure user experience.

3. Why We Use Cookies

XiteAI uses only essential cookies to ensure the Platform’s core functionality and security. We do not use cookies for marketing or advertising. Essential cookies enable:

  • Secure user authentication and session management.
  • Storage of user preferences, such as language or account settings.
  • Safe management of waitlist registrations and subscription plans for our 2026 launch.
  • Protection against security risks and unauthorized access.
  • Local and cloud storage for user interaction data, as described in our Privacy Policy.

4. Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following essential cookies to support the Platform:

  • Authentication Cookies: Enable secure logins, including third-party logins (e.g., Google, Apple, X).
  • Preference Cookies: Store settings for a personalized user experience.
  • Security Cookies: Protect against unauthorized access, in line with our security protocols.
  • Performance Cookies: Collect anonymized data to improve Platform performance.

Local storage is used to cache user preferences on your device, reducing server-side data storage.

5. Managing Cookies

Essential cookies are required for the Platform to function and cannot be disabled. You may adjust your browser or device settings to manage cookies, but this could limit certain functionalities. Refer to your browser’s help resources for guidance. For details on data control, see our Privacy Policy.

6. Third-Party Services

Our Platform integrates third-party services, such as payment processors, which may use their own essential cookies. These are governed by their respective policies, which we recommend reviewing. XiteAI is not responsible for third-party cookie practices.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy as our Platform evolves toward its 2026 launch. Significant changes will be communicated via email, website notifications, or app updates. Continued use of the Platform indicates acceptance of these updates.
